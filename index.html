From the Great Outdoors…

Conshohocken is an open secret as a central and beautiful location for avid and casual bike riders alike. Two of Montgomery County’s often traveled bike trails come together in Conshohocken – the Schuylkill River Trail that connects Valley Forge and Philadelphia and the Cross-County Trail that connects Conshohocken and Plymouth Meeting.

And if you venture a little further out on the Schuylkill River Trail, when you hit Valley Forge park, you will find even more trails for biking, hiking, fishing and picnicking. For more outdoor ideas in and around Conshohocken, take a look at this local guide and of course, feel free to ask us for recommendations.
